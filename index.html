Legendary Craftsmanship

At Exman Legendary Barbershop, located in Kamawewe, Mgobodz, we believe grooming is an art form. Each cut is a reflection of precision, attention to detail, and deep understanding of style.


Our space at the Mawewe Chiefs Sport Ground has become more than a barbershop—it's a community institution where quality craftsmanship meets genuine connection.


With years of honed expertise, we specialize in precision fades, clean line-ups, and tailored styles that complement each client's unique features and personality.
